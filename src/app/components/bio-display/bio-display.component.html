I've been everywhere... more or less. After growing up right here in St. Louis I moved to Chicago and then New York to study dance. I attended the School of American Ballet, the feeder school for the New York City Ballet, before heading west to Seattle to dance with the Pacific Northwest Ballet. From there I moved to New Zealand to dance with the Royal New Zealand Ballet before returning to the States, dancing with SD | Prism Dance Theatre and Emery LeCrone Dance before focusing more on my singing. I began auditioning for musicals and joined the first national tour of Aladdin. After a year of high kicks in Agrabah I moved on to Jesus Christ Superstar's 50th anniversary tour. The pandemic gave me time to breathe, think, and reevaluate which has lead to my current incarnation: a man with a guitar in his hand and a song on his lips.
